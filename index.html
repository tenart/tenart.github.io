MY EDUCATION

Vietnam Australia International School

Saigon, Vietnam

Established in 2004 and licensed by the Vietnamese Government, VAS is a private school group with the national education system offering both the K-12 Vietnamese National and the Cambridge International education programmes.

Saigon South International School

Saigon, Vietnam

SSIS is a private international school offering an American standards based curriculum for grades EC-12. They offer both the IB diploma program and AP coursework, and is fully accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. The school is also member of East Asia Council of Overseas Schools, National Association of Independent Schools, and Mekong River International Schools Association (MRISA). SSIS was recently designated an Apple Distinguished School.

The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

Chapel Hill, North Carolina

UNC is a public research university located in Chapel Hill, North Carolina, United States. It is the flagship university of the University of North Carolina system. After being chartered in 1789, the university first began enrolling students in 1795, which also allows it to be one of three schools to claim the title of the oldest public university in the United States.
